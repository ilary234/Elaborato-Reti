A strange Kpop group

NCT (an acronym for Neo Culture Technology) is a South Korean boy band formed and managed by SM Entertainment.
Introduced in January 2016, the group consists of 25 members divided into six different sub-units.
They have been cited as K-pop's "most experimental boy band" due to their concept of having limitless members.
Although each sub-unit promotes separately, NCT has united four times as a group to record four full-length albums.
